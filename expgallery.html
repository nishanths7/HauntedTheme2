This is the Berkyn Manor, a.k.a. the Bull Manor, nicknamed after a rather unfriendly bull that guards its premises. Several hundred years old, this beautiful English mansion has been home to various families before its last inhabitant, Ernest Rayner, died on November 11, 1987, aged 96. The place remains abandoned ever since.
