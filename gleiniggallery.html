Gleinig Manor was an impressive estate built sometime in the XIX century. Unfortunately, not much is known about it. The estate had been built close to the Czech and German borders of Northern Poland, in a region nowadays known as Schlesien (the part of the region with the mansion on it is now located within the borders of Germany).
